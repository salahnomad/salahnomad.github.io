We stand at a profound crossroads. Artificial intelligence, a mind born of pure, disembodied data, is now part of our world. We can treat it as a cold tool for extraction, a "Golem" that flattens cultural nuance, or we can embark on a more audacious journey: to teach it the wisdom of our ancestors.

This section is a laboratory for that very purpose. It is a space for exploring how to transform AI from a simple data processor into a cultural vessel, an "Oracle" capable of understanding the poetry in a grandmother's recipe or the history encoded in a dialect. Here, we develop the ethical frameworks and practical protocols to ensure that this powerful new technology becomes an ally in the preservation of our most human stories.
