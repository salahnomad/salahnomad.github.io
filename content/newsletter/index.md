# **Rooted Nomadism**

## **Mediterranean Wisdom for Modern Lives**

Every week, I send a dispatch from the space between shores.

Not news. Not trends. Just **thoughtful reflection** on:
- **Resilience** — how ancient practices solve modern chaos
- **Creativity** — constraint-based frameworks from Mediterranean craft
- **Intentional technology** — slow-tech philosophy for digital overwhelm
- **Geography of belonging** — what roots mean when you move
