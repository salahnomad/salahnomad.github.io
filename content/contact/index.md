### A Note on Correspondence

To honor our mutual time and energy, please consider the following before sending your message:

-   **Kindred Spirits First:** Every response is crafted personally. I value meaningful dialogue over transactional exchanges.
-   **Clarity is a Compass:** The clearer your query, the better I can navigate a response.
-   **Patience is a Virtue:** I reply to every genuine message, but like a good tagine, a thoughtful response takes time to prepare.

This harbor is a place for building bridges, not for pushy sales pitches or spam. If your message is sent with intention and respect, it will be received in the same spirit.
