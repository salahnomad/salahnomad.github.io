My story begins with the hiss of a radio and the scent of mint tea in a dim Moroccan kitchen. It was there I learned that borders are illusions and that the world is a network waiting for us to transmit. This belief has been my compass through a twenty-year journey across continents and technologies.

This section chronicles the key moments of that odyssey: the failures that became my greatest teachers, like the ghost of *Ritual Hammam Europe*; the crises that forged my resilience, like a global pandemic that locked down the world but opened my soul; and the serendipitous encounters that revealed my path. This is not just my autobiography; it's a testament to the idea that our truest identity is often found in the spaces between the worlds we inhabit.
