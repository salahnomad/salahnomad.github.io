My journey began with the hiss of a shortwave radio in a dim Moroccan kitchen. It was there, straining to hear voices from distant continents, that I first learned a fundamental truth: the world is a network, and our greatest challenge is to find the signal in the noise.

This belief has been my compass through a twenty-year odyssey from the olive groves of Ksar El-Kébir to the digital shores of Málaga. This isn't just a travelogue. It’s the story of how my life's fractures were reassembled into a philosophy: **Rooted Nomadism**.

The articles below are the pivotal chapters of this journey. They are the stories behind the frameworks, the crises that forged the wisdom.
